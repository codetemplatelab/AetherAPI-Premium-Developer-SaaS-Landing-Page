# Product Pricing: AetherAPI

## Suggested Pricing (Indian Market)

### Basic License: ₹1,299
- Use for 1 personal or commercial project.
- Lifetime updates.
- Standard support.
- No redistribution.

### Extended License: ₹4,999
- Use for unlimited personal or commercial projects.
- Use in a SaaS product where end-users are charged.
- Priority support.
- Full design source files (Figma link if applicable).

## Pricing Logic
The pricing is positioned as a "Premium Mid-Tier" product. 
- **Value Proposition:** Saves approximately 40-60 hours of design and development time.
- **Competitive Edge:** Inclusion of Three.js and high-end Framer Motion animations justifies the premium over standard Bootstrap or basic Tailwind templates.
- **Market Fit:** Affordable for solo founders and small startups in India, while maintaining a high perceived value for international buyers.
